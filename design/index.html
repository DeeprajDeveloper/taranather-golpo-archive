Design Philosophy

Minimal surface, maximum atmosphere. Cards are the protagonist. One moment of strangeness per page.

  • Minimal surface, maximum atmosphere. Nothing decorates unless it unnerves.
  • Cards are the protagonist. The layout exists to frame them.
  • Legibility over mood. Mood is created through color and type, never at the cost of readability.
  • One moment of strangeness per page. A texture, a flicker, a red bleed — used once, used well.

Color System

Dark mode is the designed experience — candlelight in a dark room. Light mode is fog mode: pale, sickly, overcast.
